The basics

Why partner with Layer 7

Your clients are being asked to prove their security. Auditors want a penetration test. Insurers and tenders want Cyber Essentials or Cyber Essentials Plus. Enterprise customers want ongoing assurance that risks are being managed. So they turn to you.

Building all of that in-house rarely stacks up. Credible testing needs CREST and Cyber Scheme qualified testers, kept current. Certifying to Cyber Essentials properly means being a licensed Certification Body, not sub-brokering it through someone else and hoping. Managing exposure across a client estate means running an exposure-management platform and knowing how to interpret it. Few MSPs can justify all three.

Partnering closes the gap in one move. We are a single specialist partner who can test, certify and manage, all to a standard your clients' auditors will accept, all delivered through you.

We do all three, and we do them as one partner. A single agreement. FAQ

Partner Programme questions

Will you sell to my client directly?

No. You keep the client relationship, the renewal and the branding. We deliver the work with you or behind you, and we never approach or sell to your client without your agreement. It is written into our partner agreement.

Can I start with one service and add others later?

Yes. Most partners start with the service their clients are asking for most, often penetration testing or Cyber Essentials, and add the others as demand grows. It is one agreement and one partner either way.

Can the work carry my branding?

Yes. By preference we co-deliver, with both names visible; or we white-label it as yours alone where a client needs us out of sight. Your call, per engagement.

Are you actually a Cyber Essentials Certification Body?

Yes. We have been an IASME-licensed Cyber Essentials Certification Body since the scheme launched in 2014, for both Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus. Your client is certified by a licensed body, not sub-brokered through a third party.

What qualifications do your testers hold?

Our testers hold individual CREST and Cyber Scheme qualifications, both recognised by the NCSC. All testing is delivered in-house, never offshored or subcontracted.

How does the managed security side work if I already run managed IT?

It sits alongside what you do. We add the specialist exposure-management layer, continuous vulnerability management, patching, attack surface management and Microsoft 365 monitoring, that most MSPs do not run in-house. We complement your stack; we do not replace it or take it over.

How does partner pricing work?

Clear, transparent pricing, tailored to you. Once scope is agreed we quote a fixed price per engagement with no hidden extras, so you can quote your client with confidence. No published wholesale list, because we build pricing around your business.

Where are you based, and who will I deal with?

We are based in the North East and work with partners across the UK. You deal with our own specialists directly, not a broker desk or a portal.
