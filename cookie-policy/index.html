Cookie Policy

The cookies and similar technologies this website uses, why we use them, and how you can manage them.

This policy explains how Layer 7 Ltd handles cookies and similar technologies on this website. Please read it alongside our Privacy Policy, which explains how we handle personal data more generally. If you have any questions, contact us at hello@layer7.uk.

What cookies are

A cookie is a small text file that a website transfers to your browser and stores on your device, so the site can recognise your browser when you return. A cookie typically contains the name of the domain it came from, its lifetime, and a value, usually a randomly generated unique number. Cookies cannot, by themselves, identify you personally. Similar technologies, such as local storage, perform comparable functions.

Cookies on this site

This website does not set any cookies. It is a static site with no login area and no contact form (you reach us by email), and we use no advertising or cross-site tracking. We do not store cookies or similar technologies, such as local storage, on your device.

Analytics

We do not use any website analytics, and we do not track how individual visitors use the site. If we ever introduce analytics or other cookie-based technologies, we will update this policy first, and where consent is required we will ask for it before any such technologies are used.

Log files

Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) processes standard technical information, such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address and browser type, in server logs in order to deliver and secure the site. This is a normal part of how any website is served. It is not used to identify you personally or to track you across other websites.

Managing cookies

Because this site sets no cookies, there is nothing here for you to manage. You can still control or block cookies for all websites through your browser settings if you wish.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the cookies and technologies we use change. Any changes will be posted on this page.
